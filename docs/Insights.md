# Marketing & Customer Value Insights

This document summarizes the key findings from the Marketing Acquisition and Customer Value dashboards. The analysis is based on a total spend of **$75.70K**, resulting in **$2.62M** in total revenue with a healthy overall **ROAS of 34.62**.

## 1. Marketing & Acquisition Insights (Dashboard 1)

### 📈 Top Performing Channel: TikTok Ads
*   **Highest Efficiency:** TikTok Ads is the standout winner with the highest **ROAS (47.20)** and the lowest **CAC ($18.54)**.
*   **Volume Driver:** It successfully acquired the largest share of customers (**1,355 customers**) while maintaining high efficiency.
*   **Revenue Leader:** Generated the highest total revenue at **$1.19M**, proving it is both a scale and efficiency leader for this dataset.

### ⚠️ High-Cost Channel: Facebook Ads
*   **Highest Acquisition Cost:** Facebook Ads has the highest **CAC ($36.05)**, which is nearly double that of TikTok.
*   **Lowest ROAS:** It delivers the lowest return on spend (**24.23**), suggesting that current ad creative or targeting on this platform may need optimization.

### 🔍 Under-Utilized: Google Ads
*   **Untapped Potential:** Despite having a very strong **ROAS (37.94)** and a competitive **CAC ($23.02)**, it has the lowest spend (**$3K**). There is a significant opportunity to scale this channel to drive more volume.

---

## 2. Customer Value & Behavior Insights (Dashboard 2)

### 💰 High-Value Customer Base
*   **Healthy CLTV:** The average **Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV) stands at $873.55**, which is roughly **34 times the average CAC ($25.23)**. This indicates an extremely profitable business model with high sustainability.
*   **Strong AOV:** The **Average Order Value (AOV) of $262.06** shows that customers are willing to spend significantly in a single transaction.

### 🔄 Retention & Loyalty
*   **Consistent Purchase Frequency:** Customers across all channels show a stable **Purchase Frequency of 3.33 orders**. 
*   **Quality Consistency:** Interestingly, while acquisition costs (CAC) vary greatly by channel, the **CLTV remains consistent (around $871-$875)** across all platforms. This means that while some channels are more expensive to acquire from, the *quality* of the customer (how much they spend over time) is remarkably similar.

---

## 3. Strategic Recommendations

1.  **Reallocate Budget:** Shift a portion of the budget from **Facebook Ads** (lowest ROAS) toward **TikTok Ads** and **Google Ads** to maximize total revenue without increasing total spend.
2.  **Scale Google Ads:** Increase investment in Google Ads significantly. Since its ROAS is the second-highest but its spend is the lowest, it is the most logical place for growth.
3.  **Optimize Facebook Strategy:** Investigate why Facebook's CAC is so high. Experiment with new ad formats or audience segments to bring the acquisition cost closer to the $25.00 average.
4.  **Loyalty Programs:** Since the **Purchase Frequency** is consistent but could be higher, implementing a loyalty or "refer-a-friend" program could push that 3.33 average closer to 4 or 5, significantly boosting CLTV.
